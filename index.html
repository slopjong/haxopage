Haxogreen 2012

July 26th-29th

HaxoGreen is a summer camp with hackers and hackerspace-folks from all over Europe to meet for an informal exchange of knowledge.

We combine socialising in the green nature with education covering topics about security, open source and hardware development. The whole camp site will be one big hackcenter with technology understood as a passion wherever bits, bytes and resistors are found.

Whether you're hacking on your project, looking for new and exciting ideas, socialising with others or just craving to share 3 midsummer nights outdoors, HaxoGreen is the place to be.

On this third iteration of our 4-day outdoor summer camp we welcome every hacker, artist and geek. But you don't necessarily need to be into technology, we also welcome all other inquisitive people from around the globe just getting an idea of the hackerspace movement and talking about experiences.

Get an impression of a previous edition by having a look at these pictures or watching this TV spot.

The registration for 2012 is live at the camp wiki!

If you want to participate with your lecture, panel discussion, workshop or lightning talk, just submit your proposal.
