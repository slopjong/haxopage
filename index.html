Haxogreen 2012

July 26th-29th

After more than 80 satisfied campers in 2010, the long awaited third iteration of HaxoGreen, in its 2012 edition is finally here! After a very successful start into the forays of international hackcamps and hackerfests, hackerspaces and hackerspace-folks from all over Europe and beyond will meet again for an informal exchange of knowledge on the this third iteration of our 4-day outdoors summer-camp.

Whether you're hacking on your project, looking for new and exciting ideas or just craving to share 3 midsummer nights outdoors, socialising with other hackers, artists and geeks, HaxoGreen is the place to be. No need to be a 1337 H4X0r, we welcome all inquisitive people from around the globe.

See the call for proposals or head straight on and submit your proposal.
Registration for 2012 is live at the camp wiki!
