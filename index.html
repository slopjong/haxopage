Haxogreen

Hackerspaces and hackerspace-folks from all over Europe met for an informal exchange of knowledge on the haxogreen summer camp organized by syn2cat and c3l in 2009 and 2010.

The event had the flair of a convention with talks, workshops and the whole camp site was one big hackcenter. Technology as a passion was present wherever bits, bytes and resistors were found.

We also talked about our experiences and ideas regarding the hackerspace movement.

The haxogreen wasn't just pure fun but also a four days event with lots of education in software and hardware hacking. Get an impression of the haxogreen 2009.
